üé≤ Basic Rules

  • Each player starts with 3 cards in hand

  • Choose 1 card to return to your hand
  • The remaining card battles against your opponent's card

  • Loser loses 1 health point
  • First player to lose all health points loses the game

‚è∞ Timer System

  • 20 seconds to select and place 2 cards on the field
  • 10 seconds to choose which card to return to hand
  • If time runs out, the system will auto-select cards for you

üÉè Card Strategy

  • You can place duplicate cards (e.g., 2 Rock cards)
  • Think about your opponent's possible moves
  • Balance between offensive and defensive strategies
  • Use the return phase to keep your best card for battle

üéØ Winning Strategy

  • Pay attention to your opponent's field cards
  • Try to predict what card they'll keep for battle
  • Use the timer to your advantage - don't rush decisions
  • Remember that you can see your opponent's field cards before choosing which to return

üéÆ Controls

  • Click cards in your hand to place them on the field
  • Click field cards to return them to your hand
  • End Turn button when you're ready to proceed
  • Reset Selection to clear your field and start over
